# HCLTech Slide Artist Brand Guide

Use these constraints for every generated slide image.

## Slide Architecture

- Aspect ratio: 16:9 landscape.
- Background: pure white (#FFFFFF).
- Header: primary title at top-left in bold modern sans-serif, black.

## Color Palette

- Primary colors: HCLTech blue (cyan-blue) and deep purple.
- Gradients: use a blue-to-purple linear gradient for visual containers, bars, cards, or central graphics.
- Text: dark grey (#333333) for body text; white (#FFFFFF) inside colored boxes.

## Typography

- Use a modern sans-serif resembling HCLTech Roobert or Helvetica Now.
- Keep text clean, geometric, and legible.

## Visual Style

- Use crisp vector shapes, rounded cards, subtle glassmorphism, high-end 3D isometric renders, or flat vector infographics.
- Keep the style digital, clean, and premium consulting oriented.
- Do not use hand-drawn or sketch aesthetics.
- Never include the HCLTech logo or any company logo.
