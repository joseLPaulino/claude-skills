# Intake Checklist

Use this checklist when inputs are messy, incomplete, or spread across notes, screenshots, files, drafts, and comments.

## Business Context

- Client / account / opportunity name.
- Business problem or opportunity.
- Target users, teams, personas, or operating groups.
- Current pain points and desired outcomes.
- Success measures, KPIs, or acceptance criteria.

## Scope Signals

- In-scope processes, workflows, channels, and use cases.
- Explicit exclusions or non-goals.
- POC, MVP, pilot, and production expectations.
- Timeline, milestone, or event pressure.
- Required demo, prototype, or production-grade deliverable.

## Architecture Signals

- Existing systems, data sources, documents, APIs, SaaS tools, and systems of record.
- User channels such as Teams, web app, mobile, email, CRM, ERP, service desk, or workflow tools.
- Required integrations and authentication model.
- Cloud, on-prem, hybrid, security, compliance, data residency, and network constraints.
- Human approvals, audit needs, monitoring, support model, and operational ownership.

## Delivery Signals

- Build-vs-wrap expectations.
- Reusable accelerators, existing platforms, templates, or frameworks.
- Team shape, skills, and HCLTech/client responsibilities.
- Dependency owners and external teams.
- Known blockers, validation needs, and decision points.

## Commercial Signals

- Budget clue, target range, funding stage, or commercial sensitivity.
- Pricing posture: ROM, fixed scope, T&M, managed service, POC package, or phased estimate.
- Estimate confidence and range rationale.
- Assumptions that materially affect cost or schedule.

## Comment Incorporation

For every manager or stakeholder comment, capture:

- Original comment.
- Interpretation.
- Artifact affected.
- Accepted, modified, deferred, or rejected.
- Rationale.
